About Us

Founded in 1980 in Dammam, Hassan Bin Ali Al Sanad & Partners Co. is a leading supplier of premium fruits and vegetables. With deep market experience and a reputation for trust, we serve the Eastern Province with high-quality products and a newly launched delivery service.

Our Products

  • Fresh Vegetables (Tomatoes, Potatoes, Peppers...)
  • Leafy Greens (Mint, Parsley, Coriander...)
  • Seasonal and Imported Fruits (Bananas, Apples, Watermelons...)

Our Services

We offer daily market sourcing, quality sorting, custom order preparation, and professional delivery across Dammam, Al Khobar, Dhahran, Qatif, and more.
